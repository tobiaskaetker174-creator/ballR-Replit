# Design System Document: "Iron & Moss"

## 1. Overview & Creative North Star: "The Earthbound Arena"
This design system rejects the sterile, neon-drenched aesthetics of typical sports apps in favor of a grounded, premium, and organic feel. It is inspired by the intersection of industrial strength ("Iron") and natural resilience ("Moss"). The objective is to create a high-contrast, immersive environment that feels like a night match under professional floodlights on a perfectly maintained pitch.

---

## 2. Color Palette (High-Contrast Earthy)

### Core Backgrounds
- **Base (Deep Charcoal):** `#141312` - A warm, near-black that provides more depth than a standard neutral black. Used for the primary app background.
- **Surface (Iron):** `#201F1E` - A slightly lighter, metallic charcoal for cards, containers, and elevated sections.
- **Overlay/Hover:** `#363433` - Used for subtle state changes or tertiary background elements.

### The "Moss" Accents (Primary & Secondary)
- **Primary (Woody Green):** `#2D5A27` - A deep, forest-inspired green used for high-impact buttons and active states.
- **Secondary (Moss Glow):** `#A1D494` - A vibrant, desaturated lime-green used for typography, statistics, and critical UI indicators (e.g., Elo ratings, success icons).

### Supporting Colors
- **Text (Earthy Parchment):** `#E6E2DF` - A soft, high-contrast off-white that reduces eye strain compared to pure white.
- **Text (Muted Clay):** `#8C8782` - A warm gray for secondary labels and metadata.
- **Separation:** `#42493E` (20% Opacity) - Subtle borders and dividers.

---

## 3. Typography: "The Kinetic Slab"
- **Primary Font:** **Inter** (or a similar high-performance Sans-Serif like Roboto).
- **Headings (Display):** Bold to Extra-Bold, tight letter-spacing (-0.02em), often uppercase for section headers and brand elements.
- **Numbers:** Monospaced or Tabular Figures are preferred for statistics (Elo, rankings) to ensure vertical alignment and clarity.
- **Hierarchy Scale:**
  - **Hero (Elo):** 48px, Bold, Moss Glow.
  - **Title (Screen):** 32px, Extra-Bold, Earthy Parchment.
  - **Subtitle (Card):** 20px, Bold.
  - **Body:** 16px, Regular, Muted Clay for labels.

---

## 4. Visual Components & Styling

### Container Aesthetics (The "Iron" Shell)
- **Corner Radius:** `16px` (Large) for primary cards; `8px` (Medium) for buttons and smaller UI elements.
- **Borders:** No heavy borders. Use subtle tonal differences (Surface vs. Base) or a very thin `1px` border in `#42493E` at 20% opacity.
- **Elevation:** Avoid traditional drop shadows. Use "Tonal Layering" (stacking slightly lighter shades of charcoal) to create depth.

### Interactive Elements (The "Moss" Touch)
- **Primary CTA Buttons:** Solid Woody Green (`#2D5A27`) background with Bold Earthy Parchment text. Include a subtle "Book Now →" arrow for directionality.
- **Filter Pills:** Outline or subtle Surface background with Moss Glow text for active states.
- **Badges:** Small, rounded chips (radius `999px`) using translucent versions of Moss Glow for skill levels (e.g., "Intermediate").

### Imagery & Media
- **Venue Photography:** High-resolution, wide-angle shots of green pitches, ideally during "golden hour" or under floodlights.
- **Treatment:** Subtle dark overlay at the bottom of images to ensure white text remains legible.

---

## 5. Key Design Principles
1.  **High Contrast, Low Strain:** High accessibility through contrasting greens on dark charcoal without using "vibrating" neon colors.
2.  **Information Breathing Room:** Use generous padding (8px grid base) to ensure data-heavy screens (like Profile/Stats) feel organized, not cluttered.
3.  **Celebratory Progression:** Use Moss Glow gradients and visual podiums for rankings to make achievements feel "earned" and premium.
